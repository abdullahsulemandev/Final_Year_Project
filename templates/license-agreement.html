Privacy Policy

At SilverScreen IQ, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

We collect data to improve your experience and to provide personalized services. This includes, but is not limited to, information you provide during registration, such as your name, email address, and payment details. Rest assured, your information is stored securely and is not shared with third parties without your consent.

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please do not hesitate to contact us. Your trust is important to us, and we strive to maintain transparency in all our dealings.
